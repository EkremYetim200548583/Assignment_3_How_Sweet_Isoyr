Discovery

Turkish delight dates back to Anatolia in the 15th century and gained fame during the Ottoman Empire in the 17th century. The double roasted variant originated in Konya in 1960.

Taste and Benefits

This delight boasts a natural pistachio taste, made with simple ingredients and offering a healthy, energy-boosting treat, perfect with coffee.
